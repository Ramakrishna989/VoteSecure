Traditional Voting Methods

Physical verification using identity cards and paper ballots is common in traditional voting systems. These methods, though reliable, often lead to long queues, logistical challenges, and increased operational costs.Paper ballots and ballot boxes ensure a physical record of votes but are time-consuming and prone to human error.

Technological Advancements

With AI-powered face recognition, voting becomes secure and convenient. Fraud is reduced, and transparency is enhanced through real-time verification and authentication.
